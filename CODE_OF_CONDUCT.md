# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
the Operant project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1, available at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/. Please read
it for the full statement of expected behavior, unacceptable behavior, and the
enforcement guidelines maintainers follow.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, give and accept constructive
feedback gracefully, and focus on what is best for the community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (the repository, issues,
pull requests, and any other official channel) and also applies when an
individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainer at work@tomcupr.com. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to
respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.
